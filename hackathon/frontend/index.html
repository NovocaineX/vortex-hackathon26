Cyber-Forensic Methodology

Adhering to strict digital forensics and cybersecurity guidelines

Forensica AI operates using a zero-trust verification model. Upon upload, documents are subjected to multi-layered cryptographic and visual inspections to ensure absolute authenticity.

  • Structural & Metadata Inspection: We extract and verify latent metadata, validating EXIF data, software signatures, and modification timestamps against expected baselines.
  • Error Level Analysis (ELA): JPEG compression footprints are rigorously compared across the image surface to detect localized re-saves indicative of tampering or tampering footprints.
  • Optical & Geometric Validation: Pixel distributions, font glyph contours, and layout grid alignments are cross-correlated utilizing computer vision to identify digital splices and cloned regions.
